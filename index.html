About

Welcome to my portfolio! I’m Josiah, a carpenter/welder in Randolph County. With a passion for furniture, I strive to provide practical as well as aesthetically pleasing works for my clients. I have a background in welding and building furniture from a renowned company based in Boone, NC, which has equipped me with a diverse skill set in manufacturing skillfully crafted pieces of furniture. Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working with several local businesses and entrepenuers, allowing me to hone my craft and push creative boundaries. When I’m not honing my woodworking craft, you can find me tinkering with welding projects or building upon my backyard homestead. I'm always looking for inspiration in the world around me and I'm always excited for a new challenge. Thank you for visiting my website! I’m excited to share my work with you and hope we can connect soon.
